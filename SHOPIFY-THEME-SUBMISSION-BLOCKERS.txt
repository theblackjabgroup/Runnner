❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Can I submit with inline styles?
A: Yes, but it's not recommended. Inline event handlers (onclick, etc.) 
   will cause rejection.

Q: How long does Theme Store review take?
A: Typically 2-4 weeks, sometimes longer.

Q: What if my theme fails review?
A: You'll get feedback on what to fix. Fix issues and resubmit.

Q: Do I need to fix ALL inline styles?
A: No, but fix inline JavaScript handlers (onclick, etc.). Those are 
   critical blockers.

Q: Can I use jQuery?
A: Yes, but modern vanilla JS is preferred by Shopify.
